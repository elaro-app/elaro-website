How It Actually Works

Three things happen:

Add your stuff

Quick and easy. Course, date, time, done. Takes maybe 10 seconds. No complicated forms, no overwhelming options—just the basics.

We remind you

Notifications before deadlines. Review reminders at the right times. You don't have to think about it.
